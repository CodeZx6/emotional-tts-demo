Representation Steering for Emotional Robot Speech

Deploying inference-time emotion control on an Ameca humanoid robot

NeurIPS 2026 — Anonymous Submission

Overview

We deploy our dual-space representation steering method on an Ameca humanoid robot (Engineered Arts) to evaluate whether inference-time emotion control transfers to a physical embodied agent. Audio is pre-generated using our method (F5-TTS backbone) and played through the robot with synchronized lip movement (via phoneme-to-viseme mapping) and emotion-matched facial expressions (60+ facial actuators).
